My Time at Mercedes Benz Financial Services

My Overall Experience



Girls Get IT helped me learn lots of new skills. I learned how to interact in a corporate setting. I learned how to set up meetings with other people and talk to them professionally. I also learned technical skills in DevOps, TechOps, DSC, IT Security and Data Analytics.

Through Girls Get IT I met a ton of amazing people that were welcoming and fun to work with. They made my experience here at MBFS invaluable and unforgettable. I had thought working meant sitting by yourself and staring at a computer but through this program I realized that working meant interacting with people and collaborating to learn from each other and create a great product. My view of the workplace changed from a dreaded place to be into a fun, relaxed environment.
