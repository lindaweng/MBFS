WIN Mentor Program

My Experience



The WIN mentor program was really helpful. I got to explore outside of IT with my mentor, Dana, who works in governance. We also had three lunch and leanrs with our mentors.

In the first one, we got some tips about how to write a resume and how to answer questions in a job interview. It was really informational and I'll defintely use it to update my resume.

In the second lunch and learn, we talked about personal branding and we learned about the Johari window. We did a self assessment of our own personality and got feedback from all of the other girls to see what was in our open, blind, and hidden quadrants (of course we don't know what's in our unknown quadrant). In the third lunch and learn, we learned about how to protect ourselves on social media, especially on Facebook.
