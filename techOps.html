TechOps

My Experience



I learned how to monitor apps using app dynamics. The techOps team monitors all of the applications for MBFS, about 80 some of them (combining to 160 for both Stage and Production Environments)! They set thresholds to get warning and critical alerts when there are too many errors for an application. Then they notify the team that made that application when any errors arise.

I also helped the team make monitoring spreadsheets for all of the applications in production. I plugged in the thresholds for the critical and warning alerts.
